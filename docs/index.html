Untwist Your Thoughts

A self-help companion based on CBT principles. Spot thought traps, track your mood, breathe through tough moments — all in under 2 minutes.

Features

  • Mood Check — slider-based mood recording in 10 seconds
  • Thought Unwinder — 4-step guided reframing process
  • Unwinding Now — instant calming with guided breathing
  • 10 Thought Traps — learn common thinking patterns
  • Insights — mood trends and activity stats
  • Breathing Exercise — 4-7-8 technique with visual guidance
  • Crisis Support — emergency hotlines always accessible

Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. No accounts. No cloud. No third-party analytics. No data collection of any kind.

Important Disclaimer

Untwist is a wellness and self-help tool based on CBT principles. It is not a substitute for professional therapy, diagnosis, or medical advice. If you are in crisis, please contact a mental health professional or call emergency services.
