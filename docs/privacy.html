Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

Untwist is designed to work entirely on-device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. Your mood entries, thought records, breathing sessions, and any notes you write never leave your device.

Data We Collect

None. Untwist does not collect any data from you. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • No crash reporting to third parties
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No health data shared with HealthKit or any external service

On-Device Storage

All app data (mood entries, thought records, breathing sessions, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is:

  • Protected by your device's passcode and encryption
  • Included in your device backups (iCloud or local)
  • Permanently deleted when you uninstall the app or use "Delete All Data" in Settings

Third-Party Services

Untwist uses zero third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or cloud services. The only external communication is:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates
  • Phone calls: If you choose to call an emergency hotline from the crisis screen, your device's phone app handles the call — Untwist does not log or record this action

Crisis Detection

Untwist includes an on-device keyword detection feature that identifies crisis language in text you type. This processing happens entirely on your device. No text is ever sent to any server. No record of crisis detection events is stored. The feature simply presents emergency contact information when certain keywords are detected.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification scheduling is handled entirely by iOS on your device. No push notification server is involved. No notification data is collected.

Children's Privacy

Untwist is rated 12+ and does not knowingly collect any data from children or anyone else.

Your Choices

  • Delete all data at any time via Settings → Delete All Data
  • Disable notifications at any time via Settings
  • Uninstall the app to permanently remove all data

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at osman.seven97@icloud.com.
