## Design Notes
- Screens 1 & 5 are dark (purple bookends) — strong visual framing
- Screens 2, 3, 4 are light variations — smooth gradient flow when swiping
- "UNTWIST" (not "UNTANGLE") — brand name consistency
- Tag lines are ALL CAPS, small font
- Line 1 is uppercase bold, line 2 is accent-colored italic feel
- CTA buttons have pill shape, matching theme colors
- All text is LEFT-ALIGNED (not centered) — more modern/editorial feel
